WHAT I DO

Kathy's Florals specializes in creating the perfect ambiance for your celebrations. Our teams may be small but they are mighty. All floral arrangments are created using the freshest cut flowers in the market. With the heat produced in Southern California, our stems are stored in a cool temperature to avoid wilting. Then, the arrangements are hand-delivered and managed prior to the beginning of the celebration. Below is a little summary of what I do in a year.
